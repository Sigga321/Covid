Coronavirus disease

COVID-19

KEEP THE WORKPLACE SAFE

PREACTICE GOOD HYGIENE

Stop handshakes
Use another noncontact method of greeting
Wash hands with soap and water for 20 seconds
Cover coughs and sneezes
Surfaces like doorknobs, tables, desks and handrails regularily.

BE CAREFUL WITH MEETINGS

Use videoconfrencing or calls for meetings when possible.
Where possible, hold meetings in open, Well-ventilated places.
Consider adjusting or postponing large meetings or gatherings.

HANDLE FOOD CAREFULLY

Limit the sharing of food.
Ensure cafeteria staff and their close contact practice strict hiegene.
Wash hands, utensils and containers.

STAY OR GO HOME IF...

If you feel sick.
You have a sick family member at home
Increase ventilation by opening windows or adjust airsystems
